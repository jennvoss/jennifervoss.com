Jennifer Voss

About

Jennifer is a lead senior consultant with SunGard Advanced Technology Services. Based in Philadelphia, she is responsible for building customer-facing products that reach millions of users.

Jennifer has been developing for the web since 2006 and has specialized in front-end development for large scale applications with a focus on JavaScript.

She holds a Bachelor of Science in Studies of Media Art & Design and a double minor in Digital Media and Screenwriting from Drexel University. She is a co-host of the Polymer podcast and an organizer and frequent speaker at the Philly Front-End/UX meetup.
